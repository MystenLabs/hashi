hashi_guardian_init

Expand description

Drive a fresh hashi-guardian from heartbeating-only to fully-initialized. Scrapes the on-chain HashiCommittee, generates BTC master + Shamir shares in memory, then runs OperatorInit -> GetGuardianInfo -> ProvisionerInit until the guardian reaches the secret-sharing threshold.
