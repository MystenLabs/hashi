pub fn mock_logger_capturing() -> (GuardianS3Client, CapturedPuts)

Mock S3 logger that captures every PutObject’s (key, body) into the returned Vec. Lets tests assert on what was written. Body is captured via match_requests (same Mutex side-channel trick as mock_logger_with_layout).
